        Japan Premium Golden Route – 6N / 7D · 25–31 October 2026

        Day
        1

        Delhi → Tokyo

        Departure from Delhi to Tokyo by flight. Arrival at Tokyo Airport. Meet & Greet by local representative. Private transfer to hotel. Check-in. Free time. Overnight in Tokyo.
        Meals: Dinner

        Day
        2

        Tokyo City Tour

        Breakfast. Full-day Tokyo sightseeing by private coach — Asakusa / Sensoji Temple, Tokyo Skytree photo stop, Shibuya Crossing, Harajuku / Takeshita Street. Lunch at local restaurant. Overnight in Tokyo.
        Meals: Breakfast + Lunch

        Day
        3

        Mt. Fuji / Lake Kawaguchi Tour

        Breakfast. Full-day excursion to Mt. Fuji area — Mt. Fuji 5th Station or viewing area (subject to weather), Lake Kawaguchi, Oshino Hakkai. Lunch included. Return to Tokyo. Overnight in Tokyo.
        Meals: Breakfast + Lunch

        Day
        4

        Tokyo → Kyoto / Osaka

        Breakfast. Transfer to Kyoto / Osaka area by Private Coach and/or Bullet Train, subject to final route and group size. Evening free for shopping. Overnight in Kyoto / Osaka.
        Meals: Breakfast + Dinner

        Day
        5

        Kyoto Cultural Tour

        Breakfast. Full-day Kyoto sightseeing — Kiyomizu Temple, Fushimi Inari Shrine, Arashiyama Bamboo Forest. Dinner at hotel. Overnight in Osaka / Kyoto.
        Meals: Breakfast + Dinner

        Day
        6

        Osaka Tour + Influencer Meet & Greet Event

        Breakfast. Osaka sightseeing — Osaka Castle photo stop, Dotonbori, Shinsaibashi. Overnight in Osaka.
        Meals: Breakfast + Dinner
