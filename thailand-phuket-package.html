        Amazing Thailand with Xploroo Creators

        5 Days / 4 Nights across Pattaya (3 Nights) and Bangkok (1 Night) — Coral Island by speedboat, the world-famous Alcazar Show, a Bangkok city tour, and an exclusive Xploroo Creator Meet & Greet with a live quiz and prize distribution. This isn’t just a Thailand trip — it’s a Creator Travel Experience.

        Live Travel Dates: 03 – 07 October 2026
        Book Now
        Duration 5 Days / 4 Nights
        Destination Pattaya + Bangkok, Thailand
        Group Size Only 50 Pax with Creator Group
        Trip Type International · Creator Travel Experience

        Package Overview

        Xploroo’s Amazing Thailand package is a 5-day, 4-night journey across two of Thailand’s most iconic cities — three nights in beach-town Pattaya and one night in buzzing Bangkok — flying return AirAsia Economy from Delhi (DEL) into Don Mueang International Airport (DMK).

        Beyond the Coral Island speedboat tour, the Alcazar Cabaret Show and a Bangkok city tour, this is a dedicated Xploroo Creator Travel Experience: an exclusive VIP Meet & Greet with creators and influencers, a live travel quiz with prizes, and a professional photo & video session — all for a strictly limited group of just 50 travellers.

        Trip Highlights

        • Return AirAsia Economy Class flights, Delhi – Bangkok – Delhi
        • 3 Nights Pattaya + 1 Night Bangkok, with early check-in
        • Coral Island Tour by speedboat + Alcazar Show with private transfers
        • Bangkok City Tour — Golden Buddha & Mini Reclining Buddha temples
        • Xploroo Creator Meet & Greet Event with Live Quiz & Prize Distribution
        • Pictures & videos with influencers, GST included

        Flight & Hotel Details

        Flight Details

        • 03 October 2026 — Delhi (DEL) → Bangkok (DMK) · AirAsia · Economy
        • 07 October 2026 — Bangkok (DMK) → Delhi (DEL) · AirAsia · Economy

        Hotel Details

        • Pattaya — Pattaya Loft Hotel (3★), Superior Room, 3 Nights
        • Bangkok — Season Siam Hotel Bangkok (3★), Standard Room, 1 Night
        Day
        1

        Delhi – Bangkok – Pattaya

        Arrive DMK Airport ~12:00 PM with a warm welcome by the Xploroo team. Private transfer to Pattaya (~2 hours) and check-in at Pattaya Loft Hotel (3★) with early check-in. Evening free to explore Pattaya Beach, Walking Street and the local markets. Overnight at Pattaya Loft Hotel.

        Day
        2

        Coral Island Tour + Influencer Meet & Greet

        Breakfast at the hotel, then a speedboat to Coral Island (Koh Larn) for crystal waters and white sand beaches — optional parasailing, banana boat, jet ski and sea walk at extra cost. Indian buffet lunch included. In the evening, the special Xploroo VIP Meet & Greet with creators/influencers at the resort: photo & video session with influencers, a live travel quiz to win Thailand souvenirs & Xploroo vouchers, and prize distribution for winners. Overnight at Pattaya Loft Hotel.

        Day
        3

        Leisure Day & Alcazar Cabaret Show

        Breakfast at the hotel, then a free morning for shopping, spa or beach time. In the evening, the world-famous Alcazar Cabaret Show with private transfers — spectacular costumes, music & dance. Overnight at Pattaya Loft Hotel.

        Day
        4

        Pattaya – Bangkok | City Tour

        Breakfast and check-out, then a private transfer to Bangkok with an enroute Bangkok City Tour covering the Golden Buddha Temple (Wat Traimit) and the Mini Reclining Buddha Temple. Check-in at Season Siam Hotel Bangkok (3★). Evening free for shopping at MBK, Platinum, Indra Market or Pratunam. Overnight at Season Siam Hotel Bangkok.

        Day
        5

        Bangkok – Delhi

        Breakfast and check-out, then a private transfer to DMK Airport for the return AirAsia flight to Delhi — taking home memories, content and creator selfies.
