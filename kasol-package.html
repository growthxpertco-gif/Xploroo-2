        Kasol & Parvati Valley

        Riverside cafés, mountain trails and laid-back valley vibes — an off-beat Himalayan escape curated by Xploroo.

        Live Ends: 26 Aug 2026
        Book Now
        Duration 4 Days / 3 Nights
        Destination Kasol, HP
        Group Size 2 – 8 People
        Trip Type Domestic · Nature & Trekking

        Package Overview

        Xploroo's Kasol & Parvati Valley trip is built for travelers seeking mountains, rivers and a slower pace — riverside cafés, gentle treks and camping under the stars, all arranged end-to-end.

        Explore the charming villages of the Parvati Valley, trek through pine forests to Kheerganga, and unwind by the river with the valley's famous café culture.

        Trip Highlights

        • Kheerganga trek with hot springs
        • Riverside café hopping in Kasol
        • Tosh & Chalal village exploration
        • Overnight riverside camping
        • Parvati Valley scenic drives
        Day
        1

        Arrival & Kasol Exploration

        Arrive in Kasol, check into your riverside stay, and spend the evening exploring the local café scene.

        Day
        2

        Kheerganga Trek

        A guided trek to Kheerganga through pine forests, with a soak in the natural hot springs at the top.

        Day
        3

        Tosh & Chalal Villages

        Explore the scenic villages of Tosh and Chalal, with time for riverside camping in the evening.

        Day
        4

        Leisure & Departure

        A relaxed morning by the river before your private transfer to the departure point.
