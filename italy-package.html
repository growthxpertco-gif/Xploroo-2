        Day-by-Day Itinerary with Influencer Events

        Day
        1

        Mumbai – Rome | Welcome Meet & Greet

        Departure flight from Mumbai to Rome. Arrival. Meet & greet with tour rep + 4 Influencers. Transfer to hotel. Orientation drive: Colosseum, Roman Forum, Piazza Venezia, Circus Maximus.
        Evening: Welcome Dinner with Influencer Meet & Greet Session.
        Meals: Dinner · Overnight: Rome – 3★ Hotel

        Day
        2

        Rome → Tuscany | Content Day

        Breakfast. Guided city tour: St. Peter's Square, Trevi Fountain, Spanish Steps, Piazza Navona. Depart for Tuscany. Wine region drive, Tuscan estate visit, olive oil + cheese tasting.
        Influencer Event: Group content shoot at Tuscan estate with influencers.
        Meals: Breakfast, Dinner · Overnight: Tuscany – 3★ Countryside Resort

        Day
        3

        Tuscany → Pisa → Cinque Terre | Coastal Shoot

        Breakfast. Pisa: Leaning Tower, Piazza dei Miracoli. Continue to Cinque Terre. Scenic coastal drive, explore 1–2 villages, local train between villages.
        Influencer Event: Group reels + photo activity in colorful villages with influencers.
        Meals: Breakfast, Dinner · Overnight: La Spezia / Cinque Terre Area – 3★ Hotel

        Day
        4

        Cinque Terre → Milan | Shopping Day

        Breakfast. Drive to Milan. Milan City Tour: Duomo, Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, Sforza Castle, La Scala. Free time: shopping with influencers for content.
        Meals: Breakfast, Dinner · Overnight: Milan – 3★ Hotel

        Day
        5

        Milan + Optional Lake Como | Farewell Event

        Breakfast. Option A: Milan at leisure. Option B: Lake Como Excursion – Bellagio, Lake Cruise.
        Evening: Farewell Dinner + Group Q&A + Meet & Greet with all 4 Influencers.
        Meals: Breakfast, Dinner · Overnight: Milan – 3★ Hotel

        Day
        6

        Milan – Mumbai

        Breakfast. Transfer to Milan Malpensa Airport. Departure flight to Mumbai. End of Services.
