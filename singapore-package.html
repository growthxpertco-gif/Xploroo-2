        Futuristic skylines, world-class theme parks and lush gardens — a premium Singapore experience curated by Xploroo.

        Package Overview

        Xploroo's Singapore Explorer is designed for travelers who want it all — iconic skyline views, thrilling theme parks and serene gardens, all within one tightly-curated itinerary.

        From the light show at Marina Bay Sands to the rides at Universal Studios and the futuristic domes of Gardens by the Bay, every day is packed with unforgettable moments.

        Trip Highlights

        • Marina Bay Sands SkyPark & light show
        • Universal Studios Singapore day pass
        • Gardens by the Bay & Cloud Forest
        • Sentosa Island beach time
        • Guided city highlights tour
